💎 Jewel Shuffle Game 💎

How to Play

🎯

Objective: Match 3 or more jewels of the same color to clear them from the board

🔄

How to Play: Click and drag to swap adjacent jewels and create matches

💥

Special Jewels: Match 4+ jewels = rainbow candy

🌈

Rainbow Bonus: Rainbow jewels match with ANY color and give 1.5x score multiplier + 50 bonus points. Required to advance levels!

⏰

Time Limit: You have 60 seconds to reach the target score each level

🏆

Level Goal: Reach target score AND collect required rainbow candies (Level 1: 1 rainbow, Level 2: 2 rainbows, etc.)

🎵

Complete: Finish all 28 levels to become a Jewel Master!
